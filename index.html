Snap It

A local-only screenshot & screen-recording tool for QA — capture, annotate, and record straight from your menu bar.

Download View on GitHub

macOS · Windows & Linux (experimental)

What it does

✂️

Capture & annotate

Lightshot-style region capture at native resolution, with shapes, arrows, pen, color, and full undo / redo.

⏺️

Record the screen

One-key recording to native MP4 — full screen or a region, 30/60 fps, with system and mic audio.

🔒

Local-only

Everything stays on your machine. No accounts, no uploads, no telemetry — captures go to your clipboard or a folder you pick.

⌨️

Menu-bar & hotkeys

Lives in the menu bar with configurable global hotkeys — screenshot (⌘⇧9) and record (⌘⇧8).

Installing on macOS

Snap It is signed but not notarized (that needs a paid Apple Developer account), so the first launch is blocked with “could not verify… malware.” The app is safe — open it once with: 

xattr -dr com.apple.quarantine /Applications/snapit.app

…then launch normally. Or: System Settings → Privacy & Security → Open Anyway. Windows & Linux builds are experimental and unverified on real hardware.
